Mentality is Everything.

Do you recall a time when someone made you laugh or smile? Do you remember how you felt throughout the day because of that? Or how about a negative experience; have you gone through a heated argument and felt a weight on your shoulders after, as if the hike just got 2x steeper and 4x heavier? No matter how we let experiences affect us, our CHOICE to react mentally changes everything else. Being prepared mentally means having control over the way you think and react.
How do we better control our minds?

Quick tips to be better
mentally today:

1. Clear your mind
2. Manage new stress
3. Eat/Drink something healthy and filling.
4. Practice discipline
5. Seek support from peers.

How you choose to utilize these steps and other techniques is your call. I personally clear my mind through meditation, or moments of mindfulness. When you rediscover who you are that way, your cognitive abilities are enlightened. You will feel more confident. You find peace. I testify of this and also stand by healthy ways of "escapes" from the world. Music, in my own case, helps immensely.

Find what clears your mind!

Your mind will forgive you by applying discipline. Life is different when you control it more effectively. Complete a task not because you need to, but because you want it. ALWAYS see a positive side in what you do. The reward comes after all that you are able to do within your own control. Having a goal of discipline makes all the difference. Controlling myself and my actions has helped me become a new person. Life is much more productive as we learn to act.
