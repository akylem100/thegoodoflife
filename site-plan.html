Overview

Purpose

Help any reader learn specific life skills to excel in the world. This website will break down how to improve yourself as a person through means of finance, mental health, social confidence, and more. Having a dream starts you somewhere, but having a blueprint to success gets you to the finish line. "The Good of Life" will emphasize on common problems we face as people and provides a starter solution to bring anyone in the dark towards the right direction.

Audience

Any audience is welcome to visit and read my website. Primarily, however, young adults are invited to read and think about what will be presented. I believe in sharing perspectives and there's no better way to do that than one adult hearing another, and in return sharing their feedback depending on the choice of topic. It would be a bigger site goal to allow a chatbox where users can share their ideas and ways to help me improve as well. Critical thinking about each one of the topics discussed is especially welcome!

Mental Wellness

(Title) "Mentality influences everything else."
Do you recall a time when someone made you laugh or smile? Do you remember how you felt throughout the day because of that? Or how about a negative experience; have you gone through a heated argument and felt a weight on your shoulders after, as if the hike just got 2x steeper and 4x heavier? No matter how we let experiences affect us, our CHOICE to behave mentally changes everything else. Being prepared mentally means having control over the way you think and react. How do we better control our minds?

Here are some quick tips to heal/grow mentally:
1. Clear your mind.
2. Manage new stress.
3. Eat/Drink something healthy and filling.
4. Practice discipline.
5. Seek support from peers.

How you choose to utilize these steps and other techniques is your call. I personally clear my mind through meditation, or moments of mindfulness. When you rediscover who you are that way, your cognitive abilities are enlightened. You will feel more confdient. You find peace. I testify of this and also stand by healthy ways of "escapes" from the world. Music, in my case, helps immensely. Find what clears your mind!
Your mind will forgive you by applying discipline. Life is different when you control it more effectively. Complete a task not because you need to, but because you want it. ALWAYS see a positive side in what you do. The reward comes after all you are able to do with discipline. This has been my goal for 2023 - becoming more disciplined, and I can say it has helped me be a totally new person. Life is much more productive.

Financial Stability

(Title) "Financial literacy fuels freedom."
We all secretly want to live life as we please. Healthy financial habits will open the door to abundant opportunities of freedom. There is so much information available on the internet that will guide you to be better with money. Why would you need to be good with money? Is life not more than just earning income and buying goods? We all love materialistic goods; however, learning to work hard manage money may have a more eternal meaning behind it. We can become happier by knowing we're trying our best to do what's right. What is right in the financial world?

Here are some tips to help YOU avoid financial heartaches:
1.Build/Fix credit score.
2.Work beyond expectations.
3. Resist impulse buying. Do you really need that?
4. Escape debt traps (pay off all debt asap).
5. Stop spending, start investing.
6. Donate, serve the needy.

"Most people are afraid of not having money... if you focus everyday on your lack of money, then your future will give you what you focus on" - Ryan Serhant
You will likely experience less stress as you become more financially stable. This kind of education leads to more rewards as it is done. Too many people are distracted by the idea of a prosperous life, but when positive goals to manage money are done in a controlled manner, another level of satisfaction is achieved. When you take care of money, you will be able to focus on many other life obligations. You can live life differently and gain new experiences when you set yourself up financially. Most importantly, you can support those you love and also donate to needy organizations. Life is better lived with more money; just enough to support yourself and your loved ones while minimizing negative stress.
